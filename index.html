Paper Submission

   Researchers, academicians, students are invited to submit their original, unpublished research manuscripts in the area of Electrical ,Electronics and Communication Engineering, Computer Science ,Information Technology and MCA, Chemical, Aeronautical and agriculture, Civil, Mechanical Engineering Mechatronics &MBA.(refer conference topics page for more details)

Paper submission instruction:
   The conference uses MyPad Paper Submission System (ICATS'17) to manage all the work concerning the papers of the conference. The papers should be submitted through online submission system. Authors are requested to submit only high quality, original and unpublished work. All submissions should be in both pdf format & word document in English and should not exceed 10 (Ten) pages. (Initial submission will be used to review the technical content of your paper. Plagiarism is highly restricted.) Once your paper is accepted for the conference, you have to submit Final version of your paper. That will be intimated through Email.
